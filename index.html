My name is Andre Rogers ...
and I like to code.

I aim to pursue a career in game and or engine development. Developing games has been something that has gripped my attention ever since I was first exposed to video games. This is likely for two reasons: one, I enjoy playing games, where I am solving puzzles and I am engaged in something skill-based, while at the same time experiencing raw and visceral sensations; the other is my love for science, specifically math and physics. Game systems allow me to push the boundaries of of applying concepts and principles from math and science.

I am a good programmer who is able to semantically break down a problem, with perseverance and hard work. I like to program in C++. I would say that it is my favorite language. While I have been working mostly with .NET, and C#. I write C++ in my free time, and C like code when using OpenGL and GLSL, I am rather fond of playing with the graphics API. I find it very useful to have a visualization of what I have just written.

I want to immerse myself in a company with people from whom I can learn, while also contributing substantially in terms of ingenuity, creativity and originality. I am always highly motivated to take creative projects past the goal line.
